CRIME AWARNESS AND REPORT PORTAL





This portal is an initiative of Team 5 to facilitate victims/complainants to report crimes complaints online.
This portal caters to complaints pertaining to crimes only with special focus on crimes against women and children.
Complaints reported on this portal are first verified and dealt by law enforcement agencies/ police based on the information available in the complaints.
It is imperative to provide correct and accurate details while filing complaint for prompt action.
Please contact local police in case of an emergency .
ational police helpline number is 100. National women helpline number is 181.
